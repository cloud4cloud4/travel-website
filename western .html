Western Province

History

The Western Province (Basnahira) is the province with the highest population with about six million people. Located within the Western Province are Sri Jayawardenepura Kotte, since 1978 the new capital territory and the seat of the Parliament of Sri Lanka, and Colombo, a major seaport, largest city and cultural and commercial center of Sri Lanka and also the capital of the province. Almost all Sri Lankan premier educational institutions are located in the Western Province.

Attractions

Some of the top attractions places include:

  • Gall Face Green
  • Viharamahadevi Park
  • Mount Lavinia Beach
  • National Zoological Garden
  • Negembo Beach
  • Independence Square
