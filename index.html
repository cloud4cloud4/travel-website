Our Package

Our packages are suitable for all three people from budget travelers , families and luxury travelers.

Types of packages

1) City Explorer: This package includes a full-day tour of a major city, with stops at popular tourist attractions such as museums, historical sites, and landmarks. The package may also include a guided walking tour and transportation to and from the destinations.

2) Nature Escape: This package is designed for nature lovers, and includes a full-day hike or outdoor adventure, such as a guided tour of a national park or a river rafting trip. The package may also include transportation to and from the trailhead or starting point, as well as a picnic lunch.

3) Beach Day: This package is designed for beach lovers, and includes transportation to and from a popular beach destination, as well as beach equipment rentals and activities such as snorkeling or parasailing.

4)Foodie Tour: This package is designed for food lovers, and includes a full-day culinary tour, with visits to local markets, farms, and restaurants.
