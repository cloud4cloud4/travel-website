North Central Province

History

North Central Province is sparsely populated, its capital is Anuradhapura, the Sacred City of Anuradhapuraofficial website is a UNESCO World Heritage Site: Anuradhapura "a Ceylonese political and religious capital that flourished for 1,300 years, was abandoned after an invasion in 993. Hidden away in dense jungle for many years, the splendid site, with its palaces, monasteries, and monuments, is now accessible once again."
